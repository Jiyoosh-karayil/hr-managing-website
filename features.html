Core Modules

Built for compliance, designed for simplicity

Every module is designed to help UK businesses maintain the records the Home Office expects.

Employee Management

Add, edit, and manage complete employee profiles. Store personal details, job titles, start dates, and contract information in one place.

Attendance Tracking

Record daily attendance with employee name, date, start and end times. Build an auditable log that satisfies Home Office requirements.

Holiday and Leave

Employees submit holiday requests. Managers approve or decline. Full leave history is recorded automatically for compliance.

Document Storage

Upload and store contracts, right to work documents, passports, and visa copies. Everything organised per employee, always accessible.

Annual Reviews

Track employee performance reviews on a structured schedule. Maintain records of goals, feedback, and development plans.

Medical Questionnaires

Collect and store health declarations securely. Meet your duty of care obligations with proper documentation.

Notice Board

Post company-wide announcements. Keep your team informed about policy changes, events, and important updates.

New Starter Onboarding

Structured onboarding forms for new hires. Collect all required information before day one so records are complete from the start.
