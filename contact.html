Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know about HR Managing.

HR Managing is a cloud-based Human Resource Management System built specifically for UK small businesses. It helps you manage employee records, track attendance, handle holiday requests, store documents, conduct annual reviews, and stay compliant with Home Office requirements — all from one simple dashboard.

HR Managing is designed for UK small businesses, particularly those holding or applying for a sponsorship licence. If you employ staff and need to maintain proper records for compliance with the Home Office or UK employment regulations, HR Managing is built for you.

Yes. Every feature is designed to help you maintain the records that the Home Office expects during an audit. This includes attendance logs, absence tracking, right to work document storage, and complete employee profiles with visa and employment details.

Pricing is per employee per month, with volume discounts. Starter plan (1 to 10 employees) is £10 per employee. Growth plan (11 to 50 employees) is £8 per employee. Business plan (51 or more employees) is £6 per employee. All plans include every feature.
