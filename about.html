Why We Exist

Supporting UK businesses with robust HR management

As a business owner holding a sponsorship licence to sponsor migrant workers in the United Kingdom, you have mandatory sponsorship duties set out by the Home Office to comply with.

We are working day and night to support our clients to make sure they have a robust HR system in place to comply with Home Office and UK employment regulations.

The Home Office require proper employee records including attendance logs, absence tracking, right to work documents, and holiday records. Without these, you risk licence suspension, penalties of up to £60,000 per illegal worker, and potential business closure.
